Joint MSRI/SI Seminar¶

The joint MSRI/SI seminar will be Thursdays, 4:30-5:30. It will alternate between 116 and MSRI (room TBA) starting at MSRI. Co-ordinators are Thomas Bloom and James Maynard. A tradition started of nominating other people to talk, so if you want someone else to talk at this seminar about something you’d like to learn, tell the co-ordinators the topic you want to hear about.
